  3. If you agree to participate in a divorcee expense: approve it
  4. Once an expense is approved, it can't be changed


Balance a Month:

  1. When a new month starts, you can balance the previous month.

  4. You and your divorcee can now pay each other the difference in expenses, by the monthly balance summary

Expenses List Click on the Expenses tab on the right-side bar

  • Expenses

This will take you to the main expenses list.
You can filter by year, month, approved or not approved expenses, or expenses by you (or your divorcee, if you both signed up).

Edit Expense

  • Click an expense to see it's details.
  • If it's your expense, you will be able to edit or delete the expense.
  • If it's a divorcee expense, you can approve or not approve it.
  • Once an expense is approved, it can't be changed.

Balance Expenses Each expense has a "month to balance" and "year to balance". If this month was balanced, e.g. Jan,2016, then no further changes can be made to this month.

If you have an unapproved expense in a balanced month you can move it to another month. But you will not be able to edit or change the expense, as long as it's balanced month/year are of a cleared month

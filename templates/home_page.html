Benefits

An easy & calm way to manage shared expenses

Fair Expenses Sharing

You can easily track your shared expenses, and log expenses with a clean, simple web interface. Easier to remember and balance your shared expenses.

Calm Communication

The most important thing is to not let an argument about an expense to explode into hostility. See your divorcee expenses, approve what you agree to participate in, and vice versa.

Closing Things

Each month you and your divorcee clear a month. The expenses for this month are locked, and closed. So you don't need to nagotiate and re-open past issues.

Take Your Time

With DivorceCalcs you can watch your divorcee expenses and consider, check and respond, in your own time.

Financial Responsibility

Raising children requires good financial management, which is not trivial even for married couples. Divorced parents need a dedicated tool, designed for their needs.

Stay Updated

Keeping tabs on expenses during the month, instead of accumulating shared expenses which you have to pay but didn't know about

Works the way divorced parents need

Designed for remote management of shared expense

DivoceCalcs works for the divorced situation. The parents don't need, and usualy can't, share and communicate expenses daily.
With DivorceCalcs, each one logs and track his or her expenses. You see and approve your divorcee expenses in your own time, and balance these expenses month by month.

Features

One place to log and track your shared expenses

Log Expenses

Enter expenses. Add purchase details, and the expected divorcee participation

Approve Expenses

Each divorcee can review and approve the other divorcee expense, and commit to participate.

Monthly Balance

After a month finished, clear the monthly balance. Once balanced, DivorceCalcs prevents further changes to this month expenses. Use the month's summary to pay each other for that month

Reports & History

Clear summary of expenses by month, with summaries by month, divorcee and approved or non approved expenses
